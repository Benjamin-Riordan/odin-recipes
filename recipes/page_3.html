Everything But the Bagel Wrap Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large tortillas or wraps
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup everything bagel seasoning
  • 1 cup smoked salmon or lox
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup arugula or spinach
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix the cream cheese with everything bagel seasoning until well combined.
  2. Spread a generous layer of the cream cheese mixture over each tortilla.
  3. Layer smoked salmon, avocado slices, arugula, and red onion on top of the cream cheese.
  4. Roll up each tortilla tightly and slice in half.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges on the side.

Enjoy your Everything But the Bagel Wrap!

Lasagne Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagne noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagne noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sauté until softened.
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of marinara sauce. Layer 4 noodles, half of the ricotta, half of the mozzarella, and half of the meat mixture. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with remaining noodles, marinara sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.

Enjoy your homemade Lasagne!
